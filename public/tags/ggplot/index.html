Quick Intro to ggplot

February 22, 2017
R is capable of creating a wide variety of graphics and data visualizations right out of the box. It’s one of the main reasons for using R. However, base R graphics are often not the most aesthetically pleasing, and can be diffuclt to customize (especially if you’re new to programs like R that don’t use graphic interfaces). Thankfully there are many packages for making elegant data visualizations in R, and a standout among them is ggplot2.
